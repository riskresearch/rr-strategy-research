# RISKRESEARCH

Research workspace for systematic trading, portfolio construction, and risk-managed market exposure.

## Overview

RISKRESEARCH is a personal research repository built around the idea that systematic investing should be approached as a structured process rather than as a collection of isolated backtests. The aim is to study how financial ideas can be turned into explicit rules, tested under realistic assumptions, and eventually translated into implementation and monitoring frameworks.

The project is organized around a simple principle: in competitive markets, strong and persistent alpha is difficult to obtain, so research should focus on clear hypotheses, robust testing, careful benchmarking, and practical implementation. That makes the process just as important as the result.

## Research Focus

The repository currently revolves around several related themes:

- **research framework**, where the methodological foundations of the work are developed
- **equity volatility targeting**, focused on risk-managed exposure to the equity market through futures
- **alternative risk premia**, focused on systematic return sources such as trend, term structure, volatility, and momentum

Together, these areas reflect a common objective: understanding how market exposure, diversification, and systematic rules can be combined in a disciplined way.
