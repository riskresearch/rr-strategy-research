# Research Framework

This folder contains the methodological backbone of the repository.

The goal is to define how systematic strategies should be studied before they are judged by backtest performance alone. The focus is on market efficiency, hypothesis formation, validation, realistic backtesting, and the connection between research, implementation, and monitoring. 

The material here is intended to support the rest of the repository by providing a common research standard for strategy development across futures, equities, and alternative risk premia projects.
